Fly Fishing Adventures

Standing in cold rivers, watching the sunrise, and pretending every cast was on purpose. Fly fishing is a hobby that can eventually become a lifestyle.

Why I Love Fly Fishing

Fly fishing is one of my favorite ways to spend time outside. It’s a mix of patience, skill, and problem-solving. You’re constantly reading the water, adjusting your approach, and trying to “match the hatch” so the fish actually care about your fly.

Basic Setup for a Successful Day

  1. Core Gear
    • 5-weight fly rod and reel
    • Floating fly line with tapered leader
    • Waders and boots
  2. Fly Selection
    • Dry flies (Adams, Elk Hair Caddis)
    • Nymphs (Pheasant Tail, Hare’s Ear)
    • Streamers (Woolly Bugger)
